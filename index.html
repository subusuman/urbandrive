About us

Urban Drive is the newly launched B2C brand of ECOS (India) Mobility & Hospitality Pvt. Ltd. Offering Chauffeur-Driven car rental along with Self Drive car Services in over 100+ cities in India and overseas. We are also the global partner of Europcar, which is Europe’s largest and the world’s 4th largest car rental company. ECO is a leader in corporate employee transport, car rental service, and electric vehicle under ECO Electric for the last 24 years. Our vision is to provide best in class services with operational excellence by constantly investing in human capital and in house technology.

Thanks to innovative technology built in-house for a seamless experience from car search, Booking and delivery, all become touch & Hassel free. Urban Drive offers Chauffeur Driven cars from mid to luxury segment cars ranging with brands like Tata, Volvo, Imported Mercedes and Toyota Coaches with the only company offering Chrysler Stretch Limousines.

With a professional management and offices in Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, Jaipur, Mumbai, Pune, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, Hyderabad, Chennai, Bangalore, Cochin, Goa, Coimbatore, Lucknow and 80 other cities in India, we are now probably India’s largest B2B/B2C passenger transportation company.
